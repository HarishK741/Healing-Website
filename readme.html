Harish

Reiki & Subtle Energy Healing

My name is Harish. I was born in Tamil Nadu, India, and I am currently based in Erode.

I work with Reiki and subtle energy healing, supporting people in reconnecting with their inner world, and guiding other healers to find their own voice — so they can share their work in a way that feels aligned, honest, and true to them.

But how did I get here ?

A Quiet Sensitivity

Long before Reiki entered my life, my body responded to people and environments before my mind could interpret them.

Certain interactions caused immediate physical depletion without emotional conflict. Other spaces created an unexplainable sense of stability and grounding.

These responses were not imagination or emotion — they were consistent, physical signals that quietly shaped my awareness.

Observation Before Belief

Over time, I noticed that inner states often shaped external experiences more directly than logic suggested. Emotional tone and attention seemed to precede events rather than follow them.

I did not seek spirituality or explanations. Awareness developed through observation, silence, and lived experience.

How Reiki Entered My Life

In 2020, during my 10th-grade annual leave, Reiki entered my life through my mother. She sought Reiki support while navigating prolonged emotional strain within a deeply toxic environment.

What stayed with me was not a dramatic moment, but the gradual softening of her nervous system — the way calm began to replace constant tension.

Soon after, I received my first Reiki attunement under a traditionally trained master with over three decades of experience.

The Turning Point

During one of my earliest meditations — only my third experience of intentional stillness — awareness dropped into an unfamiliar depth of silence.

In that stillness, a luminous presence was felt gently resting at the crown of my head. There was no emotion, vision, or effort — only clarity and profound neutrality.

Lotus imagery arose repeatedly. I did not interpret the experience at the time. Weeks later, through Magnified Healing attunement, it aligned clearly with the compassionate current associated with Kwan Yin.

Integration, Not Identity

This experience did not become an identity or a claim. It became a responsibility.

Healing does not flow through personality or belief. It moves through presence, integrity, and restraint. I remain a practitioner, a student, and a space-holder.

Growing Into Practice

Reiki began as a private practice shared only with family and close friends. As consistency and inner steadiness deepened, others naturally began seeking sessions.

Some experienced sensations, imagery, or light. Others felt very little during the session — yet noticed emotional clarity, improved rest, or a calmer nervous system afterward.

Healing does not require sensation. It requires readiness.

Choosing Alignment

Alongside this path, I completed a B.Tech in Information Technology in 2024 and explored conventional career options.

While everything appeared stable externally, something felt misaligned internally. I chose presence over systems, depth over speed, and alignment over security.

How I Work Today

My Reiki sessions are quiet, receptive, and grounded. I do not guide sensations or suggest outcomes. Reiki works through intelligent responsiveness — not instruction.

Integration often continues beyond the session, appearing as emotional clarity, deeper rest, or subtle internal recalibration.

Ethics & Integrity

I do not diagnose or claim to heal. My role is to support the conditions in which balance can return naturally.

There are no exaggerated promises — only presence, awareness, and respect.

Who This Work Is For

This work supports sensitive and empathic individuals, those experiencing emotional strain, burnout, or energetic depletion.

No prior experience is required — only openness to rest, receive, and reconnect.
